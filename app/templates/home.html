What is G02.ME?

  • G02.ME is free URL shortening service - use it to turn any URL into a short one.
  • G02.ME URL's are only 16 characters long (for comparison, tinyurl URL's are 25 characters long) - perfect for embedding in Twitter or IM messages (e.g., http://g02.me/G)
  • Exchange comments at the top of any shared G02.ME page.
  • Each G02.ME page displays the number of viewers so you can see how often your link has been viewed.
  • Pick a username, and use it with all your comments. You can then get a summary of all your most recently shared pages.
  • Add a tag, and then click on the tag link to see the most popular URL's for that tag - it's a great way to find out what others are sharing.
* Installation instructions for Internet Explorer:
  • Right click on the G02.ME link (above)
  • Select "Add to Favorites..."
  • Click "Yes" when you see the Security Alert.
  • Select "Links" in the "Create In" dropdown.
  • Click "Add"
