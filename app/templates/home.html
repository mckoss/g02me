Community URL Sharing Service

Just drag this bookmarklet to your toolbar*:

G02.ME

  • G02.ME is free for everyone - and no need to create an account.
  • Shrink the URL address of any web page to be just 15 characters (e.g. http://g02.me/G)
    (for comparison, new tinyurl.com URLS are now 25 characters long)
  • Send your new G02.ME URL to your friends in instant messages or twitter tweets.
  • G02.ME will add a frame at the top of your shared page where you can exchange comments.
  • See how often your page is being viewed - each G02.ME page displays the number of viewers directly on the page.
Installation instructions for Internet Explorer:
  • Right click on the G02.ME link (above)
  • Select "Add to Favorites..."
  • Click "Yes" when you see the Security Alert.
  • Select "Links" in the "Create In" dropdown.
  • Click "Add"
