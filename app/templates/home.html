What is G02.ME?

  • Free - G02.ME is free hyperlink shortening service - use it to turn any link into a short one.
  • Short - G02.ME links are only 16 characters long (for comparison, tinyurl links are 25 characters long) - perfect for embedding in Twitter or IM messages (e.g., http://g02.me/G)
  • Comments - Exchange comments at the top of any shared G02.ME page.
  • Analytics - Each G02.ME page displays the number of viewers so you can see how often your link has been viewed.
  • Personalized - Pick a username, and use it with all your comments. You can then get a summary of all your most recently shared links.
  • Tags - Add a tag, and then click on the tag link to see the most popular links for that tag - it's a great way to find out what others are sharing.
* Installation instructions for Internet Explorer:

  • Select "Links" in the "Create In" dropdown.
  • Click "Add"
